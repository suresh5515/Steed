    RULES AND REGULATIONS

    Standard Rules

    • Free-hit is applicable for all kinds of no balls.
    • Minimum 4 bowlers has to be used by the teams.
    • If the non striker goes out of the crease before the ball is bowled and if the bowler hits the stump with the ball without completing the arm action the non striker will be given out.
    • If the bowler disturbs the stumps while delivering the ball that will be considered as no-ball.
    • Wicketkeeper cannot change his position behind the stumps without prior intimation to the umpire.
    • If a bowler was found guilty of bowling two hip no balls in a match he will be banned from bowling in that match.
    • If a bowler was found guilty of chucking he will be banned from bowling throughout the tournament.
    • At any circumstances runner will not be provided for any batsman.
    • The players will not allowed to play in more than one team.The player who already played in one team cannot play even as a substitute for other team.
    • The teams can avail substitutes only from committee if they lack adequate members.No batting and bowling is available for substitutes.
    • If any player is found guilty of playing for more than one team ,the team for which he is playing at that moment will be debarred from the tournament.
    • Without a proper reason a batsman cannot get Retired Hurt and the batsman can only get Retired hurt with the permission of umpires.
    • If the batsman is found guilty of running in the danger area in pitch that run will not be taken under consideration.
    • Usage of Lungi is strictly prohibited.
    • A team must be ready to play three matches in a day, if required.
    • If any match ends in draw, the winner will be adjudged by “super over”.
    • Organizers reserve the right to change the date, time and reduce the over of matches at short notice.
    • Umpire's decisions will be final throughout the tournament. Any sort of misconducts by any players will result in direct suspension.
    • After the toss, the fielding team should set the fielding positions immediately to avoid any delay to begin the match. Batsmen will come to the crease only after the field arrangement.
    • Teams are advised to avoid any unnecessary delay before or during the game to maintain our time schedule.
    • Organizing committee will reserve full authority to intervene in such case off field and umpires will have full authority to intervene in field.

    Powerplay Rules

    • Only two players are allowed outside the 30-yard circle in powerplay.
    • Only maximum of 4 players are allowed outside the 30-yard circle in non-powerplay.

    Innovative Rules

    • DOUBLE DHAMMAKA
    • Each run scored in the Double dhamakka over will be doubled(E.g if 6 runs are scored in that over that will be considered as 12 runs) subsequently for each wicket falls in that over two runs will reduced from the total.
    • CRACK JACK
    • If the batsman can crack the first ball of the innings for a six that will be considered as 10 runs.
