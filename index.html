    Kandipalayam Cricket Club (Steed Rocks)

    We steed rocks debuted in cricket tournament which took place in vaiyapamalai in 2006 under the guidance of murugayen who played a major role in mentoring us and he made us believe that we can also play in the tournaments. As we were starters at that time we didn’t taste the success immediately.But then the sparks arose in the form of Suresh and Prakash as both of them kept the Opponents wandering in Allalapuram tournament but unfournately we failed to put our name on the trophy by a very small margin.But we kept progressing from one level to other level over that period.We participated in majority of the tournaments which took place around our region and we focused more on the progress rather than thinking about the result.Atlast the day came on 2013 where we scripted a history in karadipatti by acquiring the 1st place where left handed Karthikeyan played most important innings of his life.We settled for 3rd place in the tournament which was conducted by winstar india in elanagar in 2014.We also settled for 2nd place in Steed Rocks trophy 2015 where P.Karthick,Gobi,AnandhKumar,Thanasekar played a pivotal role in anchoring the team throughout the tournament.The adrelaine still runs in our body to taste more success and we hope that we will script more history in the upcoming years.
