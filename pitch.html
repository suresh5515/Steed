    The Pitch Preparation

    Curator plays a major role in the preparation of the pitch and thankfully we got two excellent curators Gobi, Yuvraj, Vijayakumar, Vignesh and Karthick.With the limited resources available both were phenomenol in the preparation of the pitch for this year's tournament even under heavy rain ,so we would like to thank both of them wholeheartedly.And not to forget the major role played by our little brothers-the young steed rocks whom all worked all along the day to support our curator team.

    The Pitch

    The pitch looks dry and hard where the ball comes nicely onto the bat so we can expect run feast from the batsmen.The size of the ground is small compared to other grounds in the surroundings which will encourage the hard hitters to come on their own.The pitch will also provides some assistance to faster bowlers since the pitch is a slope,the ball will carry to the keeper faster than expected.The pitch will assists some seam movement for the fast bowlers and under overcast conditions the bowlers can expect the ball to move on both ways. So our pitch provides a good contest between both bat and ball.

    Excellent pitch
    Easy to take wicket
    Excellent for pace bowlers
    Adopt to innovative rules
    Great challenge in power play
    Easy to hit
