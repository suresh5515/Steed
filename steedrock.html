Steed Rocks Trophy 2016

We Steed Rocks conducts cricket matches annualy where various teams fights among themselves for the Steed Rocks Trophy. It was initiated in the year 2013 which became a grand success which was followed by 2014 and 2015.The trophy of inaugural edition was won by Maniarasu cricket team and they also successfully defended the 2014 trophy.In 2015 at somepoint it looked like Maniarasu team will make the hat-trick of trophies but the new pool of talents emerged in the form of Musiri Cricket team and they took away the 2015 trophy. 2016 edition is going to be the fourth edition of steed rocks trophy and it is going to be very competitive and interactive compared to the other 3 editions and we are the ones who always comes out with an innovative ideas to make the game more interesting and more attractive and this time too the teams can expect more innovative ideas along with ideas which where followed over the three years so we are expecting the healthy competition among the teams and we wish all the visiting team to make this edition a Rockstar.
